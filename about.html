About FilmScore

FilmScore is a project created by two students: Dima Matyushchenko and Misha Efteni.

We are aspiring Junior Developers passionate about web technologies and cinema.

Our Mission

The main goal of FilmScore is to provide a user-friendly platform for discovering, rating, and discussing movies and TV series. We aim to create a community where film enthusiasts can share their opinions and find new favorites.

We believe that sharing experiences enriches the way we perceive art, and cinema is a powerful form of it.
